After a decade of being sexually harrassed, called "sweetie" in meetings, and sometimes having to work on projects that make my heart hurt, it was time for a change. As one of only three female Creative Directors in the entire pro sports world, I've struggled to overcome this beef I had with business.

Finally, it came time to put on my boots (they're made for walking after all), grab a drop of courage, and throw my...

Middle fingers to the sky!


So, I started my own agency with a bit of spiritual Vibe, a smaller footprint and a focus on giving love + light towards others. Thankfully, a few wonderful clients and colleagues came with me (Dorothy Boyd, thank you!).

- Mia
